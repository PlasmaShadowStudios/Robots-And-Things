https://giphy.com/gifs/1mkNO6EFohivp9LOLj Tiny update: **The Move Meter** Check out the top right of that gif to see a proof of concept meter that adds visual feedback to move chaining. Each move costs "charges" to execute. A spin jump costs 4, the dive costs 1, and a kick costs 2. Staying on the ground or bouncing off stuff can refill the meter. (Also the kick got an experimental revamp that makes it more like an air dash that halts all gravity. It's like a dive that you can aim but slightly more costly.)

Hello again ! Thank you all for sticking with us these past few months, a lot has changed in my life and I briefly needed to take a step away from the project to focus on my own life.
