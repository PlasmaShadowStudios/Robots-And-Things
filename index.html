Robots & Things

A 3D Collectathon Platformer with Score Attack elements!

Chain moves to get more points as you bounce off enemies, and use your combined level high scores to unlock cool abilities!

Think you can you beat the high score of your rival, the Circlebot?

Check Out The Demo & Updates Page

Click the Demo link at the top to try the game out yourself!

Check out the updates page to see how development is going, and the developer map to see what features are coming next! If you have questions or feedback, join our discord by clicking the iconn on the far right on the nav bar!

Main Collectables

Collect Puzzle Pieces to gain a ton of points and score high in action levels! You can find them outside levels in hub areas to boost your total score as well!

Collect Gears and trade them for a variety of extra abilities to go along with your main moves.

Find Challenge Medals and collect all of them in a world to unlock a bonus level with special missions.

Move Chaining & Score Mechanics

The Move Meter lets the Blockbot chain moves in the air! As you gain more moves you'll be able to upgrade the meter! For more info check the Videos section!

Every move you perform in the air increases a combo multiplier. Bounce off enemies before you land to gain more points and keep the combo going!

Chain moves, bounce off enemies, speedrun levels, and find collectables to get tons of points! Your total score across all levels will be key to unlocking super helpful abilities.

Power Ups

Hit switches in levels to permanently activate power up panels! This one makes you zoom across areas!

Chuck bowling balls at pins, enemies, and switches. It's great for wracking up points too!

Certain bonus levels lead to unlocking really handy power ups like this one! The rocket can let you glide for a long period of time!

Customization

Use coins to buy costumes for the Blockbot in shops! Some NPCs might reward you for wearing certain things!

You can find paint buckets around the world and in shops to change the Blockbot's colours!

Trade in coins for different wacky antennas.

Collect coupons to get discounts on the above items!
